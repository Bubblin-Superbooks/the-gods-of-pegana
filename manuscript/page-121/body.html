Of Ood

Men say that if thou comest to Sundari, beyond all the plains, and shalt climb to his summit before thou art seized by the avalanche which sitteth always on his slopes, that then there lie before thee many peaks. And if thou shalt climb these and cross their valleys (of which there be seven and also seven peaks thou shalt come at )

last to the land of forgotten hills, where amid many valleys and white snow there standeth the "Great Temple of One god Only."
